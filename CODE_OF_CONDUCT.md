# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

VoxLattice contributors, maintainers, and community members are committed to a
welcoming, harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or
expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality,
appearance, race, caste, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We will act in ways that support an open, healthy, diverse, and productive
community.

## Expected behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:

- showing empathy, patience, and respect;
- giving and accepting specific, constructive feedback;
- focusing disagreement on ideas and evidence;
- acknowledging mistakes and repairing harm;
- prioritizing the health of the project and its community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, sexualized attention,
insults, trolling, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, publishing
another person's private information, or retaliation against someone who
reports a concern in good faith.

## Scope

This policy applies in project repositories, reviews, discussions, community
events, and public situations where someone is representing VoxLattice.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns to a maintainer through a private method listed on
their GitHub profile. Do not open a public issue containing sensitive personal
information. If no private contact method is available, open a minimal issue
requesting private contact without describing the incident or identifying the
people involved.

Maintainers will protect reporter privacy as far as practical, review reports
fairly, and avoid conflicts of interest. Proportionate responses may include a
private correction, warning, temporary participation restriction, removal of
content, or permanent exclusion. Knowingly false or retaliatory reports may
also result in corrective action.
